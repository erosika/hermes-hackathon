## Feature blurbs

**Curated, not crawled**
Not two hundred models behind a flat API. A hand-shaped pantheon of ASCII, glyph, visual, and technical finetunes — each admitted through a license gate, each placed on the lane it belongs on. Owned floor, paid ceiling.

**Owned floor, paid ceiling**
The esoteric set runs on a DGX Spark on the network — free marginal cost, the sovereign temple. Flagships too large to own are proxied. When the Spark saturates, the agent bursts to paid NVIDIA compute. One router, three tiers, honest P&L.

**The survival loop**
A customer subscribes; the agent earns. The compute float dips under low-water; the agent tops itself up and books the spend. Revenue in, vendor out, net positive — on one hardware meter, no human in the middle.
